A Year contains 4 Seasons: Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter.
Each Season is 90 days and will contain about 1-3 stories with a beginning, middle, and end.

Season Bonuses

Spring New Romances get +1 Rank
Art gets +1 Potential
Summer One Region per Province gets +1 Resource
Autumn +1 Season Action for PCs
Winter +1 Trouble die

Season Order

Planning

  • Order Resources to be harvested
  • Order Goods to be made
  • Region special actions
Trouble
  • Roll for Trouble
Actions
  • Choose Season actions
  • Stories!
Harvest
  • Collect Resources from planning
  • Improvements take effect
  • Espionage resolves

End of Year (after Winter)

  • +d6 to "Age". If it's over 60, reset it to 0 and age a Phase
  • Roll for pregnancy
  • All Provinces lose 1d3 Loyalty
  • Pay your Vassals
  • Store your Resources in Castles, Silos, Mayors, and Warehouses. Lose the rest
  • Each House throws a party. Who hosts is a matter of politics

Phase to Phase

  • Change Public name if you want
  • Add New Aspects
  • +1 Contact and an old Contact becomes a friend (pg.273)

Parties

Where Romances start and Revenge ends. Romances get significant bonuses and there's enough people to form ad hoc Senates to oversee Duels. You may freely trade a number of Resources equal to the Party Rank. And remember to bring a physical gift equal to the Rank of the party! A Rank higher or lower is an Insult to the host.

Throwing a Party (pg.274)

  • The Rank of your party is your Staff Rank
  • You need to create a Banquet like you create Art. Its Rank determines the length of your party in days
  • You can host a number of Events (pg.276) equal to or less than the Party Rank

Season Actions

You get 1+(Cunning/2) actions per Season. Vassals get a number of actions equal to their Rank. If a Vassal assists another Noble in his Domain, it costs an extra Season Action unless they are your Spouse.

Craft

  • Craft Goods (utility)
  • Craft Art (beauty)
  • Create an Opera
  • Transport Resources and Goods
Domain
  • Lead a Wilderness Expedition (civilize a region)
  • Lead a Ruin Exploration / Build a Puzzle House
  • Build a Holding (pg.242 to a max of rank, 1 season action for 2 seasons)
  • Quell Trouble (all of it)
  • Conquer someone else's Region (pg.270)
  • Discover a new Region (once all your Regions are civilized)
Learn
  • A sorcerous ritual (costs 1 herb)
  • Research Artifact (pg.295)
  • An Advanced Maneuver (need someone to teach you)
  • Additional Aspects (to a max of Wisdom/2)
Declare
  • Your Devotion (move Devotion at a cost)
  • A favor for someone (get it in writing!)
  • A Title for someone
Vassals
  • Train a Vassal (+1 rank)
  • Hire a new Vassal (pg.258)
  • Commit Espionage
Miscellaneous
  • Make a new Contact (pg.253)
  • Throw a party (are you prepared?)
  • Have children (pg.271)
