Loyalty and Security

Region Loyalty starts at 1. Giving it a Luxury adds 3. Giving a Province a Luxury gives 1 to every Region in it.
At 5 Loyalty, it produces an extra Resource. If at least half of a Province's Regions have this, the Province gets +1 Security.
At 10 Loyalty, it produces an additional extra Resource. If at least half the Province's Regions have this, the Province gets another +2 Security.

Spy Actions against other Regions are a Contested Risk of your Espionage Action Rank vs. the Province Security.
Espionage Action Rank = Spy Network Rank + Helper Rank. You can assign yourself, your Spouse, or your Spy Master as a helper if you wish.
Province Security = the Rank of the Spy Network assigned to it. Their Season Action may be used to double that.

Vassal Loyalty = Vassal Rank + Yearly Luxury Bonus
