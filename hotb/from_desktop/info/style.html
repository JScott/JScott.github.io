Some Common Uses for Style


Aspects

  • Refresh an Aspect's Invoke
  • Tag an Aspect (give Style)
  • Compel an Aspect (give Style)
  • Discover someone's Aspect*
  • Deny someone compelling you (2 points)
  • Give the scene a free Aspect
Other
  • Define something about the world or yourself
  • Make something happen that's not worth a Risk
  • Contribute a suggestion to another player's decision
  • Give to your favorite NPC to see him play a bigger role
  • Activate an Ancestor's Blessing
  • Create cool things "outside" the rules
