Setting up the game

Player Creation

Flip over 3 more species cards then there are players. Players somehow determine which races they want and the other 3 become NPC races.

As you pick your race, draw 2 random cards from the creation deck. These cards represent a great fate dealt to your species and you decide how they dealt with it. Your decision ripples throughout history, bestowing a minor trait to your species. When you determine the NPCs, somehow determine 1 trait for each of them.

Galaxy Creation

Somehow set up the galaxy appropriately from the tile deck. This involves placing the 3 NPC races on planets. Place each player on their homeworld along with their starting fleet as dictated by their species.

Randomly pick 3 tokens for each of the 5 technology tiers as well. These will represent the technologies available to all players in this game.

Playing the game

Play Order

At the game's start, somehow pick a starting player and go clockwise. The order from then on will be the order that players folded on their turn, from the first to the last.

Taking Actions

On each turn you have 3 actions: a Major Event, a Minor Event, and a Move Event. Alternatively, your Major Event can be traded in for two Minor events, resulting in three Minor Events and a Move Event on your turn.

Major Events can be used to engage in Battles or advance to new Technology.

Minor Events can be used to Build, play a Card, or spread Propaganda to enemy planets.
