Tips:

  • Overwhelmed by the history? Press the comma key to only see history involving you
  • Be careful who you duel! The numbers next to god names represent their power level
  • Choose your targets carefully. Other gods are protective of their followers
  • Don't neglect towns. They may forget you in time without influence
  • Fear is obedience! In a pinch, Wrath will force obedience amongst your followers
  • Spread the love. You can influence any mortal unit, not just cities

Instructions:

Before Game

  • Used the arrow or number keys to select menu options and space or enter to select them
  • The mouse wheel or - and + keys next to the backspace will change values at god creation
  • Randomize the god creation options with the comma key

In Game

  • Cities are represented by ®, settlers by %, and armies by &
  • Actions can only be selected with the number keys
  • The arrow keys will now move around your cursor instead
  • You can also click anywhere on the map to move your cursor there
  • The mouse wheel or - and + keys will change the selected action's parameters
  • Toggle the history filter with the comma key to only see events pertaining to you
  • Toggle influence vision with the period key to see how much land you influence
  • Toggle the region names with the forward slash key (/)
